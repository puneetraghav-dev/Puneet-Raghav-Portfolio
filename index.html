01. About Me

Crafting intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and scale.

I'm an AI Engineer and full-stack developer passionate about building end-to-end intelligent systems that solve real-world business problems. My expertise lies in transforming raw data into actionable intelligence through machine learning and generative AI.

On the machine learning side, I work with Python, NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn for data exploration, cleaning, and modeling. I specialize in feature engineering, algorithm selection, and hyperparameter optimization—using XGBoost, SVM, and ensemble methods to build performant predictive models.

For deep learning, I design and train neural networks with TensorFlow and Keras across diverse architectures: CNNs for computer vision, RNNs/LSTMs/GRUs for sequential data, Transformers for NLP, and ANNs for tabular data. I'm particularly skilled in Natural Language Processing, semantic search, and building RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems.

On the production side, I'm experienced in building scalable ML systems—containerizing models with Docker, orchestrating with Kubernetes, tracking experiments with MLflow, versioning data with DVC, deploying APIs with FastAPI, and managing infrastructure on AWS. I believe in end-to-end ownership: from understanding the problem, to building the model, to shipping it in production.
