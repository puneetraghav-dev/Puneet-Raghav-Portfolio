01. About Me

Turning raw data into models that make decisions.

I am Puneet Raghav, a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Vishveswarya Group of Institutions, Gautam Buddha Nagar.

My work sits at the intersection of classical machine learning and deep learning. I use Python with NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn to explore, clean, and model data — from exploratory data analysis and feature engineering to building and evaluating algorithms with libraries like XGBoost, and tuning them for better performance.

On the deep learning side, I build and train neural networks with TensorFlow and Keras, working across architectures including ANNs, CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, GRUs, and Transformers. I'm especially interested in Natural Language Processing, applying these techniques to understand and classify text.

I care about doing the full pipeline properly: understanding the data first, engineering the right features, choosing an appropriate model, and evaluating it honestly — not just chasing an accuracy number.
